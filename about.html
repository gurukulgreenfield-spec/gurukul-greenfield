About Us

Gurukul Greenfield Junior High School is committed to providing a nurturing and holistic educational environment that focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and character development. We believe education is not only about learning from books but about shaping the leaders of tomorrow.


Our Vision

To create a learning ecosystem where students grow with wisdom, values, creativity, and confidence, preparing them for a brighter and responsible future.


Our Mission

  • To deliver high-quality education with modern teaching methods.
  • To encourage strong moral values, discipline, and leadership qualities.
  • To provide a safe, inclusive, and engaging learning environment.
  • To foster creativity through sports, cultural activities, and experiential learning.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and caring teachers
  • Balanced curriculum: Academics + Values + Co-curricular activities
  • Classrooms with healthy learning environment
  • Focus on discipline, ethics, and character building
  • Regular parent–teacher interaction

Infrastructure

Our school campus is designed to provide a joyful and safe learning space. We offer:

  • Well-ventilated classrooms
  • Playground for sports and physical activities
  • Science & computer labs (progressively upgraded)
  • Library with children-friendly learning resources
  • Safe and clean environment
