Admissions

Welcome to Gurukul Greenfield Junior High School. We are delighted that you are considering enrolling your child with us.

Admission Process

  1. Collect the admission form from the school office or download it online.
  2. Submit the completed form with required documents.
  3. Attend interaction/assessment (if applicable).
  4. Pay the admission fee to confirm the seat.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport-size photographs
  • Previous school report card (if applicable)
  • Address proof of parents

Fee Structure

Please visit the school office to get the detailed fee structure for the academic year.
